Building your own Personal Computer

Most people think of computers as bland beige towers like the ones of the past but when made by ones own hands it can be an art. Custome computers are oftin an exprestion of the builder. Building our own computer is also more iconomic then baying a prebiult systom.Building your own PC is not necessarily a proposition. But if you're willing to devote the time and resources to the project, you will end up with the best possible computer on Earth for you and that will make everything else worthwhile. By researching each individual component's capabilities and limitations, you can tailor your purchases to your exact needs now and in the future. And if your requirements or your mood change tomorrow, next week, next month, or next year, you can easily pull out and replace as few as one of the pieces, and your computer is perfect for you yet again. Nothing else gives you this much control or satisfaction. You will have to sacrifice some portability, but the result will be something you can Proudly call your own as you never will be able to with an unchangeable system designed and manufactured entirely by someone else.
